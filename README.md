# 📄 PurePdf

**PurePdf** is a light, modern, and straightforward PDF viewer for Android. Built with a focus on a clean User Experience (UX), this app just does its job—opening and managing your PDF files with ease and style.

This project is an enhanced fork of the original [sav-pdf-viewer-pro](https://github.com/Sav22999/sav-pdf-viewer-pro).

## ✨ What makes PurePdf great?
- **🚀 Lightweight & Fast:** No bloat, no lag. It opens your documents instantly.
- **🎨 Modern UI:** A fresh look built with Jetpack Compose that looks great on any device.
- **🌙 True Dark Mode:** Optimized for comfortable reading, especially in low light.
- **🌟 Favorites & Bookmarks:** Easily star your most important files to find them later.
- **⚡ Grid & List Views:** Choose the layout that works best for you with a single tap.
- **🔍 Quick Search:** Find your local documents in seconds.

## 🛠 Built With
- **Kotlin** & **Jetpack Compose**
- **Material Design 3**
