Welcome to the privacy policy for **PurePdf** (the "Application"), developed as an Open Source project by **Omar Ahmed** ([@outlandishomar](https://github.com/outlandishomar)). 

> **TL;DR:** PurePdf is a 100% offline application. We do not collect, store, track, or share *any* of your personal data, app usage, or document contents. Period.

---

### 📴 1. Data Collection & Usage
**Zero Data Collection.**
The Application does not obtain, collect, or transmit any personal information when you download and use it. Registration is entirely unnecessary. 

### 📍 2. Location Tracking
**No Tracking.**
This Application does not collect precise or approximate information about the location of your mobile device.

### 🤝 3. Third-Party Access
**None.**
Since the Application collects absolutely zero information, no data is (or even can be) shared with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics, background trackers, or ad networks.

### 🚪 4. Opt-Out Rights
Because we don't collect any data, there is nothing to opt-out of! However, if you wish to stop using the Application, you can simply uninstall it at any time using your device's standard procedures.

### 👶 5. Children's Privacy
We do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. Given the offline and purely functional nature of the app, it is safe for all ages, but we encourage parents to monitor general device usage. If you have reason to believe a child has provided us with any information mistakenly, please contact us so we can resolve it immediately.

### 🔒 6. Security
Your data security is guaranteed by the architecture of the app itself. Everything processing—merging, splitting, reading—happens locally on your device. Since we do not transmit data to any servers, there is absolutely zero risk of server-side data breaches.

### 🔄 7. Policy Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for operational or legal reasons. Any changes will be posted directly on this page.
* **Effective Date:** March 10, 2026
